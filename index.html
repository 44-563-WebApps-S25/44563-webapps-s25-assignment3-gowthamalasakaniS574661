My Favorite Food: Biryani

How to Make a Cup of Tea

  1. Boil water.
  2. Add tea leaves.
  3. Let it steep:
    1. For 3-5 mins if strong tea is preferred.
    2. For 1-2 mins if light tea is preferred.
  4. Strain and serve.
